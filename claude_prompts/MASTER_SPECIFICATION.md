# Garage Hofwil AG — Master Website Specification

Version: 1.0

## Purpose

This document is the single source of truth for the Garage Hofwil AG website project.

Every design decision, component, animation, layout, page structure, visual element, and content decision must follow the principles defined in this specification.

### Priorities

1. Trust
2. Clarity
3. Professionalism
4. Simplicity
5. User Experience

If a design decision does not improve trust in Garage Hofwil AG, it should not be included.

---

## Company Overview

Garage Hofwil AG is an established Swiss automotive workshop located in Münchenbuchsee.

The company has more than 25 years of experience and specializes in repairs, maintenance, diagnostics, inspections, and servicing of all vehicle brands.

The website's primary purpose is to generate workshop and service enquiries.

Vehicle sales are secondary and will primarily link to an external platform.

---

## Brand Positioning

Garage Hofwil AG should be perceived as:

- Trustworthy
- Professional
- Experienced
- Transparent
- Personal
- Reliable
- Swiss
- Premium but approachable

Core message:

> We are the mechanic you can trust with your vehicle.

The website must not feel like:

- A tuning garage
- A racing team
- A motorsport brand
- A luxury dealership
- A startup SaaS company

Instead it should feel like:

> A modern Swiss workshop with a strong reputation and long-term customer relationships.
